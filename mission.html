    Roles and Responsibilities



    Core Team



    President

  • Formulation of society meetings and ensuring the meetings are constructive.
  • Having a general control on all the affairs of the society.
  • Leading the whole society towards progressive outcome.
  • Foremost and hard decisions must be implemented by the President.
  • Become the representative of the society.



    • Vice President

  • Becoming the alternative representative on behalf of the President.
  • Coordinating with the president in societal meetings along with the convener.
  • Examining the content of social media and emails by the media team.
  • Managing and securing society credentials.
  • Evaluating societal budget monthly.



    • Joint Secretary-

  • In overall charge of the functioning of the department allocated to him to lead.
  • Maintenance of GCR inventory and records of the budget along with the Vice-President.
  • Formulating decisions with the core members before implementation.



    • General Secretary-

  • Looking after the general consequences of society.
  • Disseminating all sorts of information and decisions to all the members.
  • Assigning work to the PR team along with the Chief Coordinator.
  • Assigning tasks to other members according to the domain they are skilled at.
  • Ascertaining members punctuality and attendance along with Chief coordinator and Joint Secretary.



    • Chief Coordinator

  • Working mostly on Ground reality and updating the higher authorities accordingly
  • Addressing all members’ doubts and providing them solutions.
  • GCR regularities, like Maintenance of the room. Listing GCR inventory and budget along with the Joint Secretary.
  • Regulating and Supervising Departmental Functions.
  • Ascertaining members punctuality and indiscipline.



    • DEPARTMENTS


    Media Head

  • Ascertaining strategic content in favour of best outcomes.
  • Examining the works of members assigned media tasks.
  • Following latest trends without forgetting the purpose which is to showcase our society talents.
  • Regulating and Supervising Departmental Functions.
  • Build an active team to ensure public engagement.
  • Manage message requests and collaboration request



    • PR Head

  • Responding to the calls, texts and emails of the society.
  • Examining the works of members assigned media tasks.
  • Working under the instructions of the Chief coordinator.
  • Aim to maintain a positive image of the society by communicating for collaborations and promotions in a positive manner.
  • Introducing ideas to the core members which are in best interest in promoting the society.
  • To keep track of all competitions and Events happening.



    • MEMBERS


    Senior Members

  • Responsible for leading projects
  • Inclined towards teaching junior members.
  • Helping in making compositions.
  • Expressing dissatisfaction among fellow members to core members for rectification.





    • Junior Members

  • Being the main voice of projects.
  • Practising and developing musical skills.
  • Working under the instructions of the Chief coordinator.
  • Executing the annual plan laid down.
