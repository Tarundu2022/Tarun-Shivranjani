Shivranjani is the music society of Ramanujan College. Founded in 2013 by a group of passionate music enthusiasts led by Mrs. Ishwanik Kaur, the society has grown to become one of the most active and popular music societies in the Delhi University's Music Circuit. The Goal of Shivranjani is to collectively make music, perform and excel individually, increasing industry connect for those who wish to pursue music either as a hobby or further as a career. With a focus on preserving and promoting the rich cultural heritage of Indian classical music and Western Music, Shivranjani provides a platform for students to learn and appreciate the intricacies of this art form. Through regular music workshops, concerts, and performances, the society aims to create a community of like-minded individuals who share a love for music. From the very beginning, the Society has its involvement in almost every event of the College like fresher parties, cultural fest, foundation day, annual day and farewells. The Society is actively involved in various social events conducted by the College like outreach programs and awareness programs. Currently the society has 3 active musical groups lead by the core team i.e "The Indian Classical Choir", "the Rock Band: Aurum", and "The Western choir", who participate in Intra as well as Inter-College Events that take place and hence making it one of the most happening and engaging Society in our college and the circuit. The society conducts one of its annual flagship events “Khamaj” in the month of April which involves various competitions and Performances based upon Indian Classical Music. Recently, in the session 2022-23, Other Events that took place were “Open Stage”, “Gaane Bhi Do Yaaro”, “Auditions”, “Orientation”, “Overdrive: The Battle of Bands”, and “Shivranjani’s showcase” and “Khamaj”. Shivranjani is one of the few societies to have won around 25 competitions in just a span of 3 months of the fest season. And the total number of competitions won, including the previous year's count, remains 30+.
