SHIVRANJANI -the Music society of Ramanujan College, started in the year 2013. The Society focuses on different genres of the music such as Indian Classical Music, Western Music and Light Bollywood. The name of the Society, Shivranjani, is derived from one of the melodious ragas of Indian Classical Music. From the very beginning, the Society has its involvement in almost every event of the College like fresher parties, cultural fest, foundation day, annual day and farewells. The Society is actively involved in various social events conducted by the College like outreach programs and awareness programs. The society was founded by Ishwanik Kaur in 2013, an esteemed alumni of our college who is now a faculty in our own college. Since, Ishwanik Kaur is a hindustani classical trained person, The name of society is based on a Raag named Shivranjani.



Events: The Society has conducted various events and workshops in the College like: Inter-college and Intra-college solo song competitions (classical and semi classical and Bollywood) Inter-college and Intra-college group song competitions (classical and semi classical and Bollywood) Antakshri Dumb Charades Music workshops for students (classical and western both)
