EVENT REPORTS



The Various Events held by Shivranjani, The Music Society of Ramanujan College in the Session 2022-23 are mentioned as follows



    Khamaj


Khamaj is the annual Flagship Event conducted by Shivranjani every year. The event is based on Indian Classical Music and includes inter college competitions and performances based upon the same. It was started in the year 2022 with 2 Competitions i.e Indian Classical Choir Competition and Solo Instrumental Competition.



The Classical Choirs of following Colleges were a part of Khamaj 2022:



  • Hindu College (Alankaar)
  • Hansraj College (Swaranjali)
  • PGDAV College (Raaga)
  • Sri Venkateswara College (Alaap)
  • Daulat Ram College (Alahyaa)
  • Kamla Nehru College (Sangeetika)
  • Maitreyi College (Riyaaz)




    • Solo Instrumentalists from Ramjas College, Hindu College, Deshbandhu College were also a part of this beautiful Event. The Event also had a melodious Jugalbandi of our two current presidents as well, Ujith Udaya Kumar on Tabla and Pranay Mathur on Flute. Both Competitions were judged by the esteemed professionals of the industry. Winners for the same were Alankaar (Hindu College) and Swaranjali (Hansraj College). Winner of the Solo Instrumental Category was Madhav Kalra (Deshbandhu College).



      Orientation 2022


    Again after three years of lockdown, Shivranjani, the music Society of Ramanujan College hosted its much awaited Orientation for the New Joinees of the Session 2022-23. New Joinees were introduced to the Society and its Proceedings. It started off with an Introductory speech by the Convenor of the Society “Dr Suman Jaiswal” followed by the speeches of our Presidents. Everyone was introduced to the Core Team and the three active Music groups of our Society i.e Indian Classical Choir, the Rock band “Aurum” and the western Choir. This followed a few live performances and screening of other award winning Performances as well. Games and other activities were also a part of the event to keep everyone engaged.

    Solo Instrumentalists from Ramjas College, Hindu College, Deshbandhu College were also a part of this beautiful Event. The Event also had a melodious Jugalbandi of our two current presidents as well, Ujith Udaya Kumar on Tabla and Pranay Mathur on Flute. Both Competitions were judged by the esteemed professionals of the industry. Winners for the same were Alankaar (Hindu College) and Swaranjali (Hansraj College). Winner of the Solo Instrumental Category was Madhav Kalra (Deshbandhu College).



    Shivranjani Showcase in Annual Cultural Fest



    For the final day of the college fest, “Josh X Sequoia” (25th March 2023), consisted of performances from Shivranjani. The first performance was a duo performance, followed by a western group performance, and then finally our college band, Aurum took to the stage and owned it. The performances lasted for around 2 hours, and was filled with rocking riffs, dynamic drum rolls, mesmerizing guitar solos, melodic flute and saxophone fills, and harmonious vocals. The audience was jamming along, singing and dancing along as the band played.



      Khamaj 2023


    We proudly presented the second edition of our esteemed annual flagship event, Khamaj, featuring two exhilarating competitions celebrating the art of Indian Classical Music. This grand culmination of musical festivities, known as the Final Musical Fest of Delhi University, took place on Sunday, 30th April 2023. We extended a warm invitation to esteemed colleges from across the esteemed DU Music Circuit to participate in this remarkable event. The opening ceremony was graced by the presence of our esteemed Convenor, Mrs. Suman Jaiswal, and the distinguished judge for the competitions, Mr. Lalit Sisodia, a renowned Classical Singer and harmonium player. The event commenced with a soul-stirring performance by the illustrious founder and alumni of Shivranjani, Mrs. Ishwanik Kaur. This was followed by captivating performances from our accomplished presidents, carrying forward the rich tradition of the flute and tabla jugalbandi. Throughout the event, the inspiring presence of Shivranjani's alumni added to its grandeur.



    The highlight of the event was the two prestigious competitions: "Rageshree: Indian Classical Choir Competition" and "Tilang: Solo Instrumental Competition." Among the distinguished music societies that participated were:



  • Alankaar: Indian Music Society of Hindu College
  • Hansraj College (Swaranjali)
  • Raaga: Indian Music Society of PGDAV College
  • Alaap: Indian Music Society of Sri Venkateshwara College
  • Samanway: Music Society of IIT Delhi
  • Dhwani: Music Society of LSR
  • Backbeat: Music Society of Ramjas College

    • The performances enthralled the audience for approximately five hours, encompassing dynamic tabla beats, mesmerizing violin solos, melodious flute and sitar renditions, and harmonious choir vocals. The event concluded with the announcement of the winners. In the Rageshree: Indian Classical Choir Competition, the victorious ensembles were Alankaar: Indian Music Society of Hindu College (1st Position) and Raaga: Indian Music Society of PGDAV College (2nd Position). In the Solo Instrumental Competition, Tilang, the deserving winners were Madhav Kalra from Deshbandhu College (1st Position) and Shubhakant Sahoo from Sri Venkateshwara College (2nd Position). The sheer talent and dedication showcased by the participants left an indelible mark on this glorious celebration of Indian Classical Music.
